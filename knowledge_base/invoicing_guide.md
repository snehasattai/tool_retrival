# Invoicing Guide

## Creating an invoice
An invoice can be created as a draft first, then sent to the recipient once
you're ready. Draft invoices can be edited or deleted; sent invoices cannot be
edited, only cancelled.

## Invoice statuses
- **draft** – created but not sent, fully editable.
- **sent** – delivered to the recipient, awaiting payment.
- **paid** – recipient has paid, or it was manually marked paid.
- **overdue** – past its due date and still unpaid.
- **cancelled** – voided, no further action possible.

## Due dates
By default, invoices are due 30 days after creation. You can customize the
due date when creating or updating a draft invoice.

## Reminders
You can send a reminder on any sent or overdue invoice. Reminders do not
change the invoice status.

## Recurring invoices
This mock system does not support true recurring/subscription invoices;
duplicate an existing invoice as a starting point for a new billing cycle
instead.
